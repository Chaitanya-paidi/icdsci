ICDSCI 2025 Call for Papers

About Conference

International Conference on Data Science and Computational Intelligence (ICDSCI) 2025 is a pivotal event that embodies the core elements of the 5th Industrial Revolution. The widespread application of data science is revolutionizing products and services, significantly impacting labor markets, economies, and societies globally. The event aims to gather scientific and technical contributions across a broad spectrum of models, tools, technologies, and applications in modern data science. The conference will cover the full range of concepts from theoretical foundations to practical implementations, including case studies, works-in-progress, and conceptual explorations.

Objectives

The main objectives of ICAIS-2024 are to:

  • Facilitate the dissemination of high-quality research findings and innovative ideas in the field of intelligent systems.
  • Provide a forum for academics and industry professionals to share their insights and experiences.
  • Encourage collaboration and networking among researchers, practitioners, and policymakers.
  • Highlight the latest advancements and future directions in machine learning, deep learning, IoT, healthcare technologies, computer vision, and optimization techniques.

Outcomes

ICDSCI-2025 aims to make significant contributions to the field of Data Science, promote collaboration, and drive innovation and progress in the development and application of advanced technologies

  • Enhanced Knowledge and Skills:
    Participants will gain a deeper understanding of the latest advancements and trends in data science, big data analytics, computational technologies, and related fields. Attendees will acquire new skills through workshops, tutorials, and training sessions.
  • Increased Collaboration:
    The conference will foster collaborations between academia, industry, and government agencies, leading to joint research projects and innovative solutions to complex problems. Networking opportunities will help participants establish new connections and partnerships.
  • Publication of High-Quality Research:
    The conference will serve as a platform for publishing high-quality research papers, enhancing the body of knowledge in data science and computational technologies. Selected papers will be featured in reputed journals and conference proceedings.
  • Showcasing Innovations:
    The latest innovations, products, and technologies in data science and computational technologies will be showcased, providing attendees with insights into cutting-edge developments and industry trends. Participants will be exposed to successful case studies and real-world implementations.
  • Policy and Ethical Insights:
    Discussions on policy and ethical considerations will provide valuable insights into the responsible and sustainable development of data science and computational technologies. Recommendations for policymakers will be formulated to address the challenges and opportunities in the field.
  • Problem-Solving and Thought Leadership:
    The conference will provide a platform for addressing pressing challenges in the field, leading to new ideas and solutions. Thought leaders and experts will share their perspectives, driving forward-thinking discussions and inspiring future research.
  • Support for Early-Career Researchers:
    Opportunities for students and early-career professionals to present their work and receive feedback from established experts. Mentoring and guidance to nurture the next generation of innovators and researchers.
  • Enhanced Institutional Reputation:
    Hosting the conference will enhance the reputation of the organizing institution as a leading entity in the field of data science and computational technologies. The conference will highlight the institution's commitment to fostering innovation and research excellence.
  • Comprehensive Proceedings Book:
    A comprehensive book of abstract proceedings will be published, serving as a valuable resource for researchers and practitioners in the field. The proceedings will document the key findings and discussions from the conference, ensuring long-term impact.

Scope

ICAIS-2024 will cover a wide range of themes, focusing on the following main areas:

  • Mathematical Foundations
  • Computational Mathematics
  • Data Science Foundations
  • Computational Intelligence
  • Computational Intelligence Applications
  • Applications of Data Science
  • Data Science and Al Ethics
  • Data Visualization and Interpretation
  • Cloud Computing and Virtualization
  • Software Engineering Practices
  • Emerging Trends
  • Industry Applications

The International Conference on Data Science and Computational Intelligence (ICDSCI) 2025 is poised to make a significant impact on the field of data science and computational intelligence and beyond.
