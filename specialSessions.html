SPECIAL SESSIONS

ICDSAI-2024 invites eminent academicians, scientists, researchers, industrialists, technocrats, government representatives, social visionaries and experts from all strata of society to work together for more meaningful and professional outcome. The ICDSAI 2024 team plans to hold workshops/special sessions on various areas of expertise, in any of the tracks based on related theme.

The Workshops/Special Sessions will be chaired by experts from different fields. The presentation of the papers will be scheduled on any day of the conference.
